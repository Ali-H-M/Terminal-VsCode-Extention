# Launch with Quick Launch

Once you have profiles saved, launching them is instant.

- Click the **$(terminal) Launch Terminal** button in the status bar, or
- Run `Terminal Launcher: Quick Launch` from the Command Palette (`Ctrl+Shift+P`)

A picker appears with all your profiles. Select one and all its terminals open immediately.

> **Tip:** Pin your most-used profile so it always appears at the top of the list.
